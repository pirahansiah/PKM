## Pricing Tiers

### Starter ($5K ARR)
- Up to 10GB knowledge base
- Basic graph queries
- Email support
- Self-hosted edge devices

### Professional ($25K ARR)
- Up to 100GB knowledge base
- Advanced reasoning engines
- Priority support
- Hybrid cloud/edge deployment

### Enterprise ($100K+ ARR)
- Unlimited storage
- Custom ontology development
- Dedicated infrastructure
- SLA guarantees
